WANTED - TAFE Instructor

SparksVR are seeking a talented individual looking to join a team of enthusiastic developers.  This individual would have a current working knowledge of the current TAFE courses Electrotechnology, Power Systems, and Electrical Fitting. 

This is a person who could work efficiently, and thoughtfully within a team to develop a training program designed to educate potential students.  This Instructor would work hand in hand with Front End developer to create VR tutorials that a student could engage in to fully understand the techniques, procedures and dangers associated with the trade.  They must be able to not only be able to teach the materials but be able to write a program for our future students.

This Instructor’s up-to-date and extensive knowledge on safety, techniques, field procedures and protocols will allow them to create a program that is easy to follow, intuitive and highly engaging for potential students, and will be a competent and insightful teacher.  A must for the job include the following skills:

  • 2-3 years' experience as a qualified TAFE Instructor 
  • Excellent communication skills 
  • Outstanding teaching experience 
  • Be an effective course instructor 
  • Ability to work well with likeminded individuals 
  • Great writing skills 
  • The ability to work long hours with dedicated deadlines in place
  • Organisational skills and drive

This person would be working 4-6 months on a program designed to educate tomorrows students on the dangers in the Electrician trade.  They would be required to work full time throughout the period and enjoy a 5% share in revenue once completed program is introduced to the public.  They would be working hand in hand with the front end developer who would oversee how the course material converted into VR production, and they would also be working hand in hand with the legal advisor who may make certain discretionary changes to the course materials.

This person would have to collaboratively worth with either person over many months to complete the course materials ready for the project to be brought to the market.

Hour worked are expected to be within the range of 30-55 hours, with an average day comprising of a 8-9 paid hours of work. A lunch break of 90 minutes is included.

This person is engaged in the project and would be open to committing time to future employment for any program changes or additions.  This person would receive: 

  • Handsome full-time payment for 6 months 
  • 5% share of revenue in the program 
  • Outstanding project and references to add to their career
